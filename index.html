    • Mars Rover
    There are currently 3 rovers roaming around the surface of the planet Mars. Their names are Curiosity, Opportunity, and Spirit. They have all made the long journey from Earth, through the stars in space, to the planet, 133.71 million kilometers away who we call Mars. These rovers took almost a year to travel through the empty darkness of space to get where they are today.
    From these rovers, we are able to collect data and organize it in such a way where YOU get to be a part of the journey. Below, you have the option to select a specific rover and alsoselect a camera from five different options. Once you have done that, you can narrow it down and select a date where pictures taken (if any) can be displayed!
