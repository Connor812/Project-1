Mars Rover Photos
There are currently 3 rovers roaming around the serface of the planet mars. There names are Curiosity, Opportunity, and Spirit. They have all made the long journey from our Earth, through the stars in space, to the planet 133.71 million kilometers we call Mars. These rovers almost took a year to travel through the emptiness of space to get where they are today.
Below you have the option to select the rover you would like to to see the pictures it's taken. You can select the camera you would like to see the pictures taken for. And lastely, you can select the date you would like to see the pictures taken on that day.
